# Colors

The color codes used in the Dutch road signs are as follows:

| Color | Hex code | RAL-code |
|---|---|---|
| White | `#d7d5cb` or `#f1f0ea` | `9002` or `9016` |
| Grey | `#8e9291` or `#4f5250` | `7042` or `7043` |
| Black | `#0e0e10` or `#2a292a` | `9005` or `9017` |
| Red | `#bb1e10` | `3020` |
| Blue | `#005b8c` | `5017` |
